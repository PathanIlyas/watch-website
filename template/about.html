Heritage & Craftsmanship

Our Story

A Century of Excellence

Founded in 1924 in the heart of Geneva, Switzerland, CHRONOS began as a small atelier dedicated to the pursuit of absolute precision. Our founder, Henri Dubois, believed that a watch was not merely an instrument to tell time, but a companion that holds the moments of a lifetime.

Today, nearly a century later, we continue to uphold his vision. Every CHRONOS timepiece is assembled by hand, requiring hundreds of hours of meticulous labor by our master watchmakers. We blend traditional techniques with avant-garde materials to create watches that are both classic and revolutionary.

The Art of Watchmaking

Our movements are the heart of our watches. Developed entirely in-house, they are recognized globally for their robustness, accuracy, and beauty. Each component, down to the smallest screw, is polished and decorated by hand.

We source only the finest materials: 18k ethical gold, platinum, and flawless diamonds. Our sapphire crystals are treated with multiple layers of anti-reflective coating to ensure perfect legibility under any condition.
